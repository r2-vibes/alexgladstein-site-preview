// Biography

A Mission for Freedom

Alex Gladstein is Chief Strategy Officer at the Human Rights Foundation, where he has worked since 2007. He has also served as Vice President of the Oslo Freedom Forum since its inception in 2009.

In his work, Alex has connected hundreds of dissidents and civil society groups with business leaders, technologists, journalists, and policymakers to promote free and open societies. His writing and views on human rights and technology have appeared in publications worldwide, including The Atlantic, BBC, Journal of Democracy, CNN, The New York Times, TIME, WIRED, and The Wall Street Journal.

He has spoken at universities and institutions including MIT and Stanford, and has briefed the European Parliament and the U.S. State Department. He frequently speaks at major conferences on the intersection of technology, finance, and human rights, exploring how open protocols like Bitcoin can be tools of liberation for the 5.7 billion people living under authoritarian rule.
