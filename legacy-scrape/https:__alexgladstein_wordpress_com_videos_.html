  • Bitcoin: A Strategic tool for civil resistance

    In this interview, Alex Gladstein joins Fadi El Salameen, Roya Al Mahboob, Meron Estefanos, and Leopoldo Lopez to discuss the importance of bitcoin as a tool to resist tyranny.

    Bitcoin has revolutionized the way people use, access and save money. Bitcoin’s ability to help freedom fighters resist authoritarian regimes and organize change is one of its most inspiring use cases.

    As authoritarian states use money and financial systems as weapons against their people in order to rule them, Bitcoin is helping activists circumvent financial scrutiny and challenge systems of political oppression around the globe.

    This panel brings together activists from around the world who are using bitcoin to fight for their freedom.

  • Smuggling the outside world into North Korea

    “The World” television program on ABC News 24 in Australia interviewed HRF chief strategy officer Alex Gladstein about the initiative “Flash Drives for Freedom,” which collects flash drives from Americans and then ship them to North Korean defector groups. These organizations will then fill the USB drives with films, TV shows, and Wikipedia pages and smuggle them into North Korea, braking Kim Jong-un’s information blockade.
